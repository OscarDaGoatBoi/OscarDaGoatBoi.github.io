My Moms Rice Recipe

Before I get started with the recipe, lets take a deep dive into the history of the orgins of rice.It is said that wild rice began to be collected and early cultivation
practised around 8000BCE.By 3,500-1200BC, dry-land rice cultivation was introduced into the Korean peninsula and Japan. Long after this, around 5000BC to 3000BC in parts of china, seeds of long-grained rice have been found in Neolithic ruins.200 BC, evidence of wet-rice appears in thailand. First milllennium BC: by this time, rice had spread into Iran and the Mediterranean via trading.After this point, rice became more
and more popular and became an internationall thing .

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 tomatoe
  • blender
  • chicken boulilon
  • oil

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. rince rice with hot water
  2. add two cups of water along with a tomato into the blender
  3. get a pan with a little bit of oil
  4. add rice into pan until brown
  5. add the blended water from earlier
  6. add 1 tablespoon of chicken bouillon
  7. cover pan with lid until rice is done

This should lead to the best , soft,flavorful, not wet too wet rice.
